夏清心 Qingxin Xia

ABOUT

I am currently a specially appointed assistant professor at Hara Lab, Osaka University, Japan. I received my Ph.D. degree from Osaka University in 2021, mentored by Prof. Takuya Maekawa. I received my M.E. and B.E. degrees from Ocean University of China in 2018 and 2015, respectively, mentored by Prof. Feng Hong.

My research interests include ubiquitous computing and mobile sensing, I work closely with Prof. Thomas Ploetz and Prof. Paul Lukowicz. I primary study human activity recognition using wearable sensor data in industrial and nursing care settings based on unsupervised and supervised learning techniques.

I am actively looking for job opportunities!
